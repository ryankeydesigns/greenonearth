Welcome to 
Green on Earth

Green On Earth is a family business that started out in late 2016, previously located up in the hills at 2 Phoenix Road with alfresco dining. We have since moved on to our current location, offering a much more comfortable environment.

We pride ourselves in using the freshest ingredients and cooking each dish to perfection. Earned with years of experience, each and every of our chefs hold multiple culinary achievements under their belt.

We started out with the simple intention of changing the world, one step at a time, by encouraging people to try our vegetarian food. Hopefully, we were able to change mindsets along the way.

Thank you for visiting Green On Earth and we hope you enjoy everything we have prepared for you.
